# Coffee Blend Recipe

## Ingredients:
- A teaspoon of "The Moon"
- Grams of "The Sun"
- A hint of "The Star"
- A little bit of "The Sky"

## How to Blend it?
I always loved to bask under the sun while watching the clouds accompany the sky. When I was a kid, the sun is the first thing I've researched about and questioned "Why is there a giant light bulb in the sky?"
