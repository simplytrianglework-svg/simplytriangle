# Coffee Blend Recipe

## Ingredients:
- A spoonful of "The Moon"
- A teaspoon of "The Star"

## How to Blend It?
Have you ever had a simple dream? A dream upon the stars, the moon, and the sky.

I think it's very relatable that as a kid--we thought that the moon is following us. How quickly I ran.

Like the sun, the moon is also interesting for me because they're like a mirror or partners! The moon and The sun. Some stories says that the two are partners or simply siblings, but eh...I'll stick to them being friends that requires a bit of space.
